About MAAYA MS-CIT Portfolio Hub

MS-CIT Portfolio Hub is a platform where students can showcase their learning journey, creative projects, technical skills, and achievements.

Empowering Students Through Digital Portfolios

Our mission is to provide every student with a professional online portfolio where they can present their projects, technical knowledge, achievements, and creative work. The platform helps students build a strong digital presence and showcase their skills to teachers, employers, and the wider community.
